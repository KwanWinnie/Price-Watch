How does the retail environment impact pricing strategies?

The retail environment also influences pricing. Supermarkets, convenience stores, and online retailers each offer different pricing strategies. Supermarkets, with their bulk purchasing power, can offer lower prices, while convenience stores often charge a premium due to the ease and convenience they provide. Online platforms may offer competitive pricing, but additional costs such as shipping fees can offset the savings.
